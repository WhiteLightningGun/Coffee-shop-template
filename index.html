About Us

An award winning business with 200 years experience in beanological analysis. We offer the finest tea and best coffee in the world as well as supplying to our direct and to wholesale customers. Discover the best tasting coffees, teas and “Coffee Lover” essentials throughout our website.

Our 50-seat cafe has been refurbished in the most eco-friendly way. With the help of the finest crafts people in the area, we have carefully restored, reused and upcycled a variety of items found at the site. We re-upholstered the sofas and chairs with our coffee sacks, uplifted and relaid the original floorboards from the main dance hall which is now our Roastery.
